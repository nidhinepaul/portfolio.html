Pan Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all- purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cup of milk
  • 3 teaspoon butter,melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

  1. shift together flour,baking powder,sugar,and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, melted butter,egg and vanilla until combine.
  3. Let batter rest for 5minutes
  4. Preheat a large skillet over meduim high heat.
  5. Spray with cooking Spray
  6. Pour batter in the hot skillet,about 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles appear on the sides and center of each pancake, 2 to 3 minutes.
  8. Flip and cook until the bottom id golden, 1 to 2 minutes
